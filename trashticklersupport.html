Q & A



Included below are some questions and answers regarding Trash Tickler. Please take a look at these before posting a support request as your question may have already been addressed. Thanks for your help.


Calendar


Q: How do I move between months or years on the calendar?
A: Swipe your finger up, down, left or right to move a month or a year at a time. Pinch to return to the current month. You will find arrows and a home control below the calendar also. Tap these to perform the same swipe/pinch functions.

Q: How do I return to the current day when the calendar displays a past or future month?
A: Tap the home button at the bottom of the page or pinch the page.

Q: How do I setup the calendar?
A: Tap the gear icon in the upper left-hand corner of the calendar page. This will bring up the configuration options for services, holidays and reminders.

Q: How do I return to the calendar page from the configuration options page?
A: There is a small calendar icon in the upper left-hand corner of the configuration options page. Tap it to return to the calendar.
 
Q: What is the red number that floats into place on the calendar for?
A: The red number is the current day of the month. It only appears in the current month. It will advance as the year progresses, disappear when the calendar is in a future or past month and reappear when you are in the current month.

Q: What are the green numbers on the calendar.
A: Those are the days you have configured as holidays observed by your service provider. They are used to adjust your calendar to coincide with the provider schedule. You can add them on the configuration options page via the grren plus button about midway down the page.

Services

Q: How many services can be configured and what is their purpose.
A: There are 3 services. Garbage collection, recycling collection and landscape collection.

 Q: Each service provides a date range but how do I block out certain weeks.
A: That is done using a holiday and a weekly handling code. More details on this can be found in the holidays section below.

Holidays

 
Q: How are holidays displayed on the calendar?
A: The day on the calendar will be shown in green.

Q: Do any of the handling codes block the green indication on the calendar?
A: No, to avoid confusion any defined holiday regardless of handling code will display as green on the calendar. If the handling code intends to remove a service you'll see the service for the holiday altered accordingly. The only item that would block a green indication is if the current day of the year is defined as a holiday. You'd still see the green though as the current day in red floats in.

Q: How do I prevent defined services for a week or more on the calendar?
A: Enter a holiday with a handling code that cancels any service through the end of the week. All services within that time period will be cleared from the calendar and no reminders will be generated.

Q: How do I move services ahead a day for a selected day or week?
A: Define a holiday with a handling code that reschedules the services one day later either for the holiday alone or starting on the holiday through the end of the week.

Q: One of my holidays is missing from the calendar.
A: Check if it is listed in the configured holidays on the configuration page. If you see it there then check to see what month, day and year it's actually scheduled to appear. Holiday configuration defaults to the month currently displayed on the calendar. As a result it might be possible to unintentionally create a holiday in the past or future that cannot be seen on the calendar without displaying the month in which it's configured.  If the date is not what you need  you can delete and re-add the date without looking through the calendar itself.

Note also - if the holiday falls on the current day the calendar will show the day as red rather than green.  You should be able to see this as the current day changes from green to red.

Q: I cannot add a holiday date due to date duplication.
A: You may only add a date once to your holiday list. Dates with a handling code indicating "...to the end of the week" actually include a range of dates as well that may be causing the duplication. When in doubt about what dates are in your holiday list check the overall list. It's sorted in date order. If a range of dates applies you'll see the  start and end of the range in the list also.

Q: I defined a holiday but it's not adjusting the service at all but it does show in green on the calendar.
A: Check if the holiday handling code is set to "No change to service schedule". If so you'll need to change the handling code.

Reminders


Q: When is a reminder issued?
A:  A reminder will appear at approximately 00:00 (based on a 24-hour clock) of the reminder day. The reminder day may be set to either "Day before service." or "Day of service.".  So, a reminder is issued a few seconds into the date on which the reminder is to be issued.

Q: What will I see when a reminder is issued?
A: You will see an on-screen reminder, hear a sound and the Trash Tickler application icon on your desktop will be badged with the number 1.

Q: I do not receive a reminder the day before service after I finish configuration when a service appears the next day.
A: Reminders are generated for dates starting the day after the current day regardless of day before or day after reminder type. If you want a reminder for a service appearing the next day you'd need to set the type of reminder to "Day of Service.".

Q: If I move my device between time zones how are reminders handled?
A: Reminders currently do not account for time zone differences. They are issued at the start of the day and can be configured a day before. This has typically been good enough for most people but let us know if this presents a problem.

Q: Can I turn off the reminders or make other reminder modifications?
A: Yes. This is done outside of the app. If open, close Trash Tickler and then locate the Settings app  for your device. Start Settings (the Setting app icon is a set of gears) and on the left you should see a "Notifications" section. Select "Notifications" and on the right the apps on your device that are part of "Notification Center" will be displayed. Find the Trash Tickler icon in the list and tap it. The view presented will allow you to make various changes such as turning off notification center (for Trash Tickler only) or changing the alert style.


Help


 Q: Where is the help in the app located?
A: All pages have a small information icon at the bottom right-hand corner of the page. Tap this icon and the help will appear.

Q: Once the help is displayed for a page how do I clear it?
A: Either tap the help itself or tap the information icon again.
