Trash Tickler for the iPad



The App for taking out the Trash


Trash Tickler is an iPad app for reminding yourself or others to take the trash out. Real trash. The kind we place in a landfill, recycle or compost.

Trash Tickler provides a calendar and reminders for the collection of regular refuse, recycling and landscape waste materials.

If your service provider's schedule changes due to various holidays you may enter these dates also using the provided list. If a date observed in your area is not included custom defined holidays may also be entered.


Calendar


Once you have entered your trash collection service schedules and observed holidays view the calendar for the current month. You'll see the scheduled services and any adjustments needed based on the observed holidays. An very small example is shown at the top-left corner of this page.

You can preview an upcoming month or year by swiping from month to month or year to year. A pinch will return to the current month.


Reminders


By default Trash Tickler will issue a reminder of the upcoming trash service due date the day before scheduled service. It can also be configured for the day of the event.
